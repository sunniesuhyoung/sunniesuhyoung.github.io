Stranded on a Planet!

Astronaut Yossarian, who led a solo expedition to the Kepler 126 star system in search of habitable planets, is stranded on the planet Kepler 126F, “Amun”, as the barrel of fuel for his nuclear fusion reactor for the return journey leaked out during unloading.

Yossarian needs Helium and Oxygen as fuel and plans to extract these gases from the atmosphere. There are only 7 gases in the atmosphere which are present in significant quantities to be crystallized: Hydrogen, Helium, Carbon, Nitrogen, Oxygen, Sulphur and Neon. Yossarian has managed to extract crystals of all 7 gases separately. But he cannot distinguish the different gases.

So, he decides to take equal volumes of each sample and sort them in terms of their increasing weight. This poses another problem as he does not have a weighing machine. However, he has used his engineering skills to create a balance scale which will allow him to compare two sets of crystals and determine which weighs more.

Yossarian has indexed the elements in the order of how he collected them. His reserves are fast depleting and if he can't make replenish the supply, the reactor of his spaceship will decay. Help him by clicking on the elements to select and then clicking on the weighing scale to compare them.

Help the stranded Yossarian find the gases that he needs to fuel his rocket and get back to planet Earth!
